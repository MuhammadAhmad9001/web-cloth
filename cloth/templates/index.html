About Us

Established in 2012, founded by Asif, is an established fashion label and one of the most prestigious and diversified in Pakistan. Sana Safinaz are true innovators and trailblazers in all their business and creative endeavors and have set the bar high for the business of fashion in Pakistan. Apparel collections include but are not limited to Pret, Diffusion and Bridal Couture lines. Included on the apparel side is the brand’s latest venture in retail. We are the only premier luxury retailer offering elegant ready-to-wear & fashion fabrics, tastefully printed and embroidered along with avant-garde silhouettes for the modern woman.
